### Insurance Coverage for Check-ups (B2B Context)

- Companies typically pay for employee health check-ups, with packages costing 10,000-15,000 baht, and companies likely getting discounts of 2,000-5,000 baht
- Example: Payataye 2 hospital offers check-ups including electrocardiogram, vision tests, blood tests, urine tests, doctor consultation, with results stored in their mobile app
- Some companies have direct billing arrangements with insurance (like April), requiring no reimbursement process

### Insurance Reimbursement Structure

- Insurance coverage for prevention varies significantly by policy type
- **Date-to-date (temporary) contracts**: Rarely include prevention coverage, as there's risk of discovering conditions that need to be covered during the short coverage period
- **Minimum formulas**: Little to no coverage (e.g., 50 euros)
- **Premium formulas**: Coverage between 150-200 euros for check-ups
- Example: For a 500-euro check-up, insurance might reimburse 200 euros, with the patient paying 300 euros out of pocket

### Distinction Between Check-ups and Medical Necessity

- **Check-ups**: Examinations done without a doctor's prescription, not necessarily medically necessary
- **Medically necessary tests**: Can be reimbursed under blood test benefits if requested by a doctor due to continuous symptoms
- The distinction is somewhat subjective, and insurers prefer to categorize preventive care as check-ups to limit risk

### Thai Regulatory Requirements

- Two types of licenses available for operating in Thailand:
    1. **Insurance license**: Covers health costs directly, requires filing with OIC (Office of Insurance Commission) with extensive guarantees of long-term viability
    2. **Health service/assistance license**: Organizes care and bills for services only, without paying health costs directly
- For an insurance license, need significant documentation and proof of ability to cover risks over time
- The advisor's experience suggests OIC licensing is very complicated due to administrative rules that function as protectionism
- Requirements for licensing include: company must be more than one year old, director must be Thai
