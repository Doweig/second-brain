## Decisions Made

### 1. Pilot Structure — Locked
- **10 customers, 1 month, 10,000 baht per customer**
- Doweig pays additional 10,000 baht per customer to Dr. C's clinic to cover cost gap
- Dr. C's margin: ~2,700 baht per customer after expenses
- All payments through Dr. C's clinic (not personal), via payment link (credit card or PromptPay)
- Payment before nurse visit
- No cash to nurses

### 2. Blood Test Package — Locked
- **Main package: ~78 biomarkers, 17,000 baht clinic price (23,139 retail = 25% clinic discount)**
- NHealth labs for everything
- **Add homocysteine** (700 baht) — important for nutritional + brain health assessment
- **Remove lipoprotein(a)** — move to add-on (one-time test, doesn't change, wasteful on repeat)

### 3. Nutrition-Focused Package — Designed (~9,000 baht target)
- Vitamin D, B12, folic acid, ferritin, CBC
- Liver: AST, ALT, GGT (not full panel — saves 260 baht)
- Kidney: BUN, creatinine
- CRP (inflammation)
- Lipid panel + fasting glucose
- Easier to market — straightforward results, actionable supplement recs
- Most healthy people will have boring full-panel results; nutrition package more likely to show deficiencies

### 4. Package Strategy
- Two tiers: ~10K and ~20K with add-ons
- Heavy metals can be cut (results often boring for healthy people)
- Omega-3 ideal but expensive (3,500–6,000 baht) — potential add-on for premium tier
- Start with pre-designed packages (customization too complex for now)

### 5. Consultation — 30 minutes
- Online via ClickMeeting (Dr. C's platform)
- 1–2 days after customer receives action plan
- Customer should read action plan first; consultation = Q&A
- Transcription possible with permission (experiment)
- Standard fee: 1,000–1,500 baht (absorbed into pilot pricing)

### 6. Intake Form — Simplified
- Basic demographics: name, DOB, gender, ethnicity, height, weight, address, email, phone, LINE/WhatsApp
- Medical history: current conditions (free text)
- Family history: chronic/serious diseases (free text, ~3 items)
- Medications + supplements with doses (critical — biohackers have long lists)
- Lifestyle (exercise, sleep, diet) — keep basic for now
- Skip medical symptom questionnaire — use in future for package recommendation matching
- Dr. C to share male + female IFM templates → Doweig to unify and simplify

### 7. Branding — Sticking with BodyCypher

## Customer Journey (Finalized)

1. Doweig does initial sales call with prospect
2. Customer pays 10K via payment link (credit card / PromptPay)
3. Customer completes registration + intake form
4. Nurse contacts customer directly (LINE/WhatsApp) to schedule morning blood draw
5. Home visit — nurse draws blood (800–1,000 baht, Sukhumvit area, 2–3 visits/day, ~1hr apart)
6. NHealth processes samples
7. Results → Dr. C's clinic email → staff verifies completeness → forwards to Doweig
8. AI extracts data + generates action plan
9. Dr. C reviews + approves action plan (email notification with link)
10. Customer receives action plan (reads it first)
11. 30-min online consultation with Dr. C (1–2 days after receiving plan)
12. Follow-up surveys after each touchpoint
13. Upsell: supplements (Shopee/iHerb for now) + prepaid next blood test at discount

## Key Insights

- **Healthy people = boring results.** Full panel mostly normal for target demo. Nutrition package more likely to surface actionable findings (vitamin D deficiency near-universal). Marketing nutrition package is easier.
- **Nutrition package = supplement upsell funnel.** People choosing nutrition-focused testing already care about supplements — natural upsell path.
- **Ratios > raw values.** Dr. C emphatic: "you know what the real problem is" when looking at ratios vs individual markers.
- **Consistency problem:** AI action plans not consistent enough yet. Dr. C wants deterministic algorithm first (range checks, flags), then AI layer on top.
- **Meal delivery partnership:** Calorie Killer and similar Thai services could customize meals to blood test results. Multi-vendor approach preferred.
- **B2B noted but deprioritized:** AI analysis tool valuable for doctors/clinics, but Doweig prefers B2C for stickiness.
- **Urgency is real:** Don't Age expanding to Thailand, NHealth fragmenting the market, people DIY-ing with ChatGPT. Window is closing.
