Summary

### Business Concept

- Blood testing and health checkup service delivered through a mobile application
- Process: Users book a nurse via app → nurse visits home to collect blood → samples analyzed at partner laboratory → results delivered in-app with personalized action plan
- App provides food recommendations, supplements, and doctor follow-up
- Pricing: 49,900 baht
- Business model involves partnering with existing clinics for blood collection, lab partnerships, and doctor services

- Expected team size: Approximately six people (three foreigners, three Thai nationals) including doctor, admin, and marketing roles
- Current committed funding: 3 million baht, with potential to increase to 5 million baht if needed for BOI eligibility

### Visa and Work Authorization

- Guillaume currently holds 20-year Elite visa (platinum) but cannot legally work without work permit
- Elite visa allows entry/exit flexibility but requires 90-day reporting
- Work permit requires company to employ Guillaume as official employee
- Digital Nomad Visa (DTV) not suitable as business operations are Thailand-based, not remote work
- Recommended approach: Establish company first, then company employs Guillaume

### Board of Investment (BOI) Exploration

- BOI typically requires investment of 5 million baht or more
- Benefits include 8-year tax incentives and import tax exemptions for machinery
- BOI requires employment of Thai nationals, with numbers depending on business size and type
- Standard requirement without BOI: Four Thai employees per one foreign employee
- BOI evaluation needed to determine if business qualifies under new technology/AI support regulations
- Requires detailed business information before determining eligibility

### Legal Requirements to Research

- Appropriate company structure for operating website, mobile app, and social media advertising
- Required disclaimers (e.g., wellness app vs. medical diagnosis)
- Contract templates for clinic partnerships
- Consent forms
- Data protection/PDPA compliance
