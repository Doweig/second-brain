### Meeting Overview

Initial consultation between Guillaume William and Christina from an advisory firm in Thailand regarding setting up a medical business. Guillaume was referred by Daniel Woods from RDA.

### Business Context & Structure Options

- Guillaume is planning a medical project primarily focused on Thailand with long-term international expansion plans
- Guillaume is married to a Thai citizen, which satisfies the 51% Thai partnership requirement for company setup
- Considering potential two-company structure: one for technical aspects (IP, applications, website) and another as a medical clinic for doctor consultations
- Open to incorporating part of the structure in Singapore depending on legal clarification

### BOI (Board of Investment) Considerations

- If business activities qualify for BOI promotion, Guillaume can access tax exemptions and privileges
- BOI-promoted activities would receive tax exemptions while non-promoted activities would be taxed as regular companies
- Can pursue a mixed structure with both BOI and non-BOI activities
- Thai spouse would hold 51% share regardless of BOI status

### Letter of Advice Service

- Legal team can provide high-level review of plans and available options
- Service includes recommendations on company structure, subsidiaries, and activity allocation across different entities
- Pricing starts at 100,000 baht but depends on scope
- Process: Guillaume submits all questions, legal team reviews and responds line by line
- Deliverable is a one-time report with one consultation session for follow-up questions
- Not included in company setup services

### Ongoing Legal Support Options

**Per Consultation:**

- One-off consultations available for specific topics (employment, contracts, etc.)
- Charged per hour starting at 10,000 baht depending on scope
- Best for occasional clarifications

**Retainer:**

- Monthly billing with capped hours
- Starts at 75,000 baht per month for maximum 20 hours
- Unused booked hours are forfeited
- Better option if frequent questions expected

### Medical Industry Expertise & Key Concerns

**Guillaume's Specific Areas of Interest:**

- PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) compliance for medical information
- Telemedicine and teleconsultation regulations
- Supplementation and custom-made supplements

**Firm Capabilities:**

- Lawyers are knowledgeable about PDPA requirements
- Support clients with supplement registration
- May need to refer to partners for highly technical medical industry matters
- Will clearly communicate which questions can be answered internally versus which require external medical expertise
