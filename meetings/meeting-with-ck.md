Date: January 29, 2026

Summary: Guillaume is developing a longevity product and sought insights on employee health checkup programs from Swin's HR experience. Swin provides HR consultancy and outsourced services, currently working with SMB clients on health check proposals. Companies typically need to see proposals before budgeting. Health checkup packages vary by age and include basic screenings, with costs around 2,500 baht per employee annually. Health insurance is separate, averaging 10,000 baht per employee per year. Summary

### Meeting Purpose

- Guillaume is building a checkup/longevity product and wanted to learn about employee health benefits, specifically health checkup programs, from Swin's HR experience
- Guillaume has a tech background with limited HR experience and sought insights into how companies handle employee health checkups

### Swin's HR Consulting Services

- Swin provides both HR consultancy (laying HR foundations for companies) and outsourced HR services
- Currently working with several SMB clients who need health check program proposals

### Health Checkup Program Process

- Companies typically don't have predetermined budgets and need to see proposals first
- Swin conducts comparisons across three hospitals and three different programs
- Hospitals offer pre-packaged checkup programs that can be adjusted for pricing
- Standard packages include blood tests, BMI, chest x-rays, and other basic health screenings
- Premium packages may include tumor markers for enhanced attractiveness
- Packages are often segmented by employee age (under 35 vs. 35 and up) with different pricing

### Pricing and Cost Structure

- Example client case: approximately 2,500 baht per employee for a comprehensive annual checkup
- Packages for under 35 years old can be as low as around 1,000 baht per employee
- Guillaume expressed surprise at the low pricing, questioning hospital profitability
- Swin explained that hospitals likely profit through volume (serving hundreds to thousands of employees) and operational efficiency (one-day service with consumable supplies)
- Direct lab testing costs more than hospital packages - comprehensive tests through N Health cost 2,000-3,500 baht
- Individual lab tests Swin regularly gets (cholesterol, HDL, LDL, CBC, FBS) cost less than 500 baht
- Health checkup programs are 100% employer-sponsored

### Health Insurance Coverage

- Health insurance is separate from checkup programs
- Average insurance cost is around 10,000 baht per employee per year (less than 1K to 10K range)
- Insurance covers private hospital services, while social security covers public hospitals

### Guillaume's Digital Checkup Concept

- Guillaume is building a digital checkup system that bypasses clinics and sends people directly to labs
- The goal is to enable more tests and more thorough checkups at reduced cost
