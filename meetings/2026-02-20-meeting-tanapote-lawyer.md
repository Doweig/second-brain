### BOI (Board of Investment) Assessment

- Guillaume's business may not qualify for BOI due to the nature of the business and timing constraints
- BOI requirements for digital industry include:
  - Employing Thai people with total salaries of 1.5 million baht per year
  - All employees must be in IT-related roles (developers, network, database personnel)
  - Minimum investment requirement (likely met, but needs clarification on fund allocation)
- BOI application process takes significant time - applications are considered only 2-3 times per year, not continuously
- **Recommendation**: Start with regular Thai company structure now and apply for BOI later when ready
- Guillaume accepted this approach

### Company Structure & Ownership

- To qualify as a Thai company, Thai shareholders must hold more than 50% of shares
- Guillaume's wife can be the majority shareholder since they are legally married
- Important consideration: Investment funds should ideally come from the Thai partner's own resources, not from the foreign spouse, to avoid nominee concerns
- However, authorities are less strict about this requirement for legally married couples
- Guillaume's wife investing as majority shareholder while Guillaume works as employee is a viable structure

### Visa & Work Permit Requirements

- Guillaume needs a visa to operate the company as a foreigner
- The Thai company can employ Guillaume once established
- Thai employee requirements for foreign work permits vary by industry - typically 1-5 Thai employees per foreign worker
- Consultant will verify specific requirements for the digital health industry

- Customer installs mobile application and books blood test

- Blood sample is sent to laboratory (e.g., Any Health)
- Lab results are displayed in the application
- AI combined with doctor consultation creates personalized action plan
- Action plan includes dietary recommendations and custom supplement recommendations
- Membership option available with monthly shipment of pre-packaged supplements
- Potential for additional paid video consultations with doctors

**Key characteristics**:

- Fully remote service - customers never visit a physical location
- Digital-first approach with online consultations
- New to Asia and Thailand markets - no direct competitors in the region

### Licensing & Regulatory Considerations

**Clinic License**:

- Whether a clinic license is needed depends on the nature of services provided
- A clinic license is required if the business will diagnose patients and prescribe medicines
- If operating a clinic, the doctor must have a valid license

**Supplements vs. Prescription Drugs**:

- Business will focus on supplements, not prescription medicines
- Supplements are generally not regulated as strictly unless controlled by FDA
- Custom supplements will be prepared and shipped to customers

**Multiple Companies**:

- Guillaume is considering whether multiple legal entities are needed (technology company vs. medical company)
- Consultant needs to research the specific regulatory requirements for this hybrid business model
