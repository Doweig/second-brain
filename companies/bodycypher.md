## Overview

BodyCypher was [[william-guillaume|Guillaume]]'s solo longevity / wellness-checkup project in Bangkok — roughly three months of work in late 2025 through Q1 2026 before merging into **Orba Health**. This stub is preserved as historical context: everything referenced across `second-brain/` (meetings, research, the V3 story deck, the V1 market sizing, the product brief) ties back to this entity.

## State

- **Dormant.** The BodyCypher entity is not actively operated; the concept and work product live on inside Orba Health.
- **Solo-project era:** ~3 months. Co-founder search ran in parallel (candidates evaluated: [[swit|Swit]], [[anton|Anton]], [[david-berghaeuser|David Berghäuser]]; medical partner [[art-chawapon-kidhirunkul|Dr. C]] joined part-time). No co-founder landed; instead the project merged into Orba Health.
- **Location:** Bangkok, Thailand.
- **Product thesis (BodyCypher era):** longevity blood panels + AI action plans + optional supplement monetization, delivered via home nurse draw → lab → AI report → doctor video consultation.
