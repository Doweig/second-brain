## Competitive Intelligence

- **Offer:** BOGO — gift a membership, get yours free
- **Price Point:** $199 per membership
- **Promotion Hook:** Valentine's Day
- **Urgency Mechanisms:** 24hr countdown banner, "Last Chance" badge, "expires in 24 hours" copy
- **Emotional Angle:** Health as act of love / accountability with partner or family
- **Target:** Existing members → gift to partner/family/friend (viral loop)
- **Social Proof:** 3 testimonials all centered on relationships (partner, sons, future children)
- **Tone:** Warm, relational, aspirational
- **Key Insight:** Framing health testing as a shared/social activity, not solo optimization. Accountability angle could be strong for retention.
