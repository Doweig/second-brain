# Superpower x Grail Galleri — Email Marketing

**Source:** Email newsletter from [[superpower|Superpower]] (members@superpower.com)
**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Subject:** "Is it cancer?" (You're not the only one Googling it.)
**Type:** Competitor email marketing / product launch announcement

---

## Summary

Superpower announcing integration of Grail's Galleri® multi-cancer early detection test into their baseline blood panel. One blood draw, 100+ biomarkers, screening for 50+ cancer types. Timed to Cancer Prevention Month. No pricing shown.

## Key Copy & Messaging

### Hook (Fear-based, conversational)
- "Weird bump on your arm... is it cancer?"
- "Stomach pain that won't go away... is it cancer?"
- Validates anxiety: 7.3M monthly Google searches containing "cancer"

### Stats Used
- ~40% of US population will develop cancer in their lifetime (ACS 2025)
- Colon and breast cancer incidence rising in younger adults
- Most routine screening is age- and risk-gated, limited to a handful of cancers

### Product Positioning
- "Cancer is changing. Screening needs to change with it."
- One blood draw → 100+ biomarkers + 50+ cancer type screening
- Pairs Galleri with Superpower baseline panel
- CTA: "Screen for cancer risk →"

### Closing
- "You can't control every risk factor. But you can control how early you look."

## Design Notes
- Single-column, clean white background, generous whitespace
- Sans-serif body + serif italic for emphasis phrases
- Orange accent (infographic gauge), warm gradient behind product image
- No pricing, no discount codes, no urgency timers

## Disclaimer
> The Galleri test does not detect a signal for all cancers and not all cancers can be detected in the blood. False positive and false negative results do occur. Should be used in addition to provider-recommended screening.

## BodyCypher Relevance
- Problem-agitation-solution copy structure — effective but edgy (cancer fear)
- "One blood draw, multiple panels" bundling = same convenience play we're building
- Galleri retail ~$949 USD — hiding price behind CTA is smart
- Seasonal tie-in (Cancer Prevention Month) without discounting
