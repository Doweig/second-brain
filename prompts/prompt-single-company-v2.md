1. **Positioning & Messaging** — Fetch their homepage, landing pages. Extract exact headlines, value propositions, how they describe themselves.

4. **Business Intelligence** — Search for funding raised, valuation, customer count, team/founders, key partnerships, scale indicators.

5. **Customer Signals** — Fetch App Store/Play Store ratings and review themes. Search for Trustpilot or other review sources.

6. **Content & Acquisition Strategy** — What content do they produce? Blog topics? Social presence? Any visible ad strategies or influencer partnerships?
