# Novi Health: Comprehensive Competitive Analysis

**Novi Health is a Singapore-based metabolic health clinic combining traditional medical care with technology-enabled coaching, targeting diabetes management and weight loss through doctor-led programs.** The company raised **$5 million in Series A funding** from Monk's Hill Ventures in November 2022 and operates from Singapore's CBD, serving "thousands" of patients since its 2018 founding by three physician-executives from Singapore's public health system.

---

## Positioning centers on "holistic, doctor-led" metabolic care

Novi Health positions itself as a **"technology-enabled preventive and metabolic health company"** that bridges the gap between episodic clinical care and continuous lifestyle intervention. Their core messaging emphasizes what traditional healthcare lacks.

**Exact homepage headline:** "Unlock The Full Picture of Your Health"

**Vision statement:** "Empowering a billion healthy years"

**Mission framing:** "At NOVI Health, we believe everyone deserves to live a life free from the burden of chronic disease. We see a future where healthcare is proactive, personalized, and empowering – where extending your healthspan, the years you live in good health, is the ultimate goal."

**Problem statement they emphasize:** "Today's typical healthcare experience sees people being treated like a transaction processed by a machine – you see a doctor, you are given specific instructions, and then you wait for your next visit."

Their six core value propositions, taken directly from their website:
1. "Expert Doctor-Led Care" - experienced doctors with personalized health plans
2. "Personalized Testing and Treatment Plans" - tailored to specific health goals
3. "Holistic Guidance and Support" - doctors, dietitians, and fitness coaches
4. "Personalized Insights and Connected Health Devices" - real-time data
5. "Clear Results, No Jargon" - actionable insights in plain language
6. "Ongoing Support, Every Step of the Way" - continuous care via app

**Key differentiator:** They repeatedly emphasize being **"where science meets genuine human connection"** and tackling "root causes" rather than just treating symptoms. The founder-doctor credibility is central to their positioning.

## Pricing spans $299 entry points to $3,399 premium programs

Novi Health operates a tiered pricing structure across three main program categories, all priced in SGD:

### Diabetes management (NOVI Magnum)
| Tier | Price | Duration | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Starter | $299 ($358.61 incl. GST) | 1 month | 1 doctor + 1 dietitian consult, HbA1c test, 1 CGM sensor, digital coaching |
| Full | $449 ($539.55 incl. GST) | 3 months | Same as Starter but 3 months of digital coaching |
| **Premium (Most Popular)** | $599 ($718.31 incl. GST) | 3 months | Same as Full + 3 CGM sensors |

**Renewal pricing:** From $359/3 months (Starter) or $479/6 months (Full)

### Weight loss programs
| Program | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Optimum Plus** | $469/month or $1,289/3 months | Doctor consult, InBody scan, GLP-1 medication, digital coaching, medication delivery |
| **Optimum 365** | $1,199/4 weeks or $3,399/12 weeks | All Optimum Plus features + weekly 1:1 personal training, supplements, dedicated dietitian |

**Eligibility criteria for weight loss:** BMI >27.5 (Asian) or >30 (non-Asian), or lower BMI with weight-related conditions.

### Health assessments (NOVI Assessment Max)
**Introductory price:** $1,099 for longevity-focused screening with 100+ biomarkers, 2 doctor consultations, and personalized action plan.

**Upsell structure:** Entry-level assessments ($299 gift certificates available) → condition-specific programs → ongoing care renewals → supplement subscriptions. CGM sensors offered at **15% discount** with active program enrollment.

**Notable:** Medication is **not included** in base program pricing—prescribed separately at additional cost.

## Product offering combines CGMs, medications, and multidisciplinary coaching

### Biomarkers tested (100+ in Assessment Max)
- **Cardiovascular:** Apo A1, Apo B, HDL, LDL, Lipoprotein(a), hsCRP, Homocysteine, NT-proBNP
- **Metabolic:** Fasting glucose, fasting insulin, HbA1c, HOMA-IR
- **Liver:** AST, ALP, GGT, Albumin, Bilirubin, Hepatitis panels
- **Kidney:** Creatinine, eGFR, Urea, electrolytes
- **Thyroid:** TSH, Free T3, Free T4
- **Hormonal:** Testosterone, FSH
- **Nutrients:** B12, Folate, Ferritin, Iron, Magnesium, Vitamin D, Zinc
- **Tumor markers:** AFP, CA 19-9, CEA, PSA/CA 125
- **Multi-cancer screening:** Lucence INSIGHT 5 (thyroid, lung, liver, pancreatic, colorectal)

The proprietary NOVI Health app includes real-time glucose visualization, food/meal logging (with new AI-powered feature), activity tracking, weight tracking, direct messaging with care team, personalized insights, CGM data integration, and appointment scheduling.

**Availability:** iOS (requires iOS 15.6+, 47.1 MB) and Android (1K+ downloads)

### AI and personalization claims
Novi Health claims "personalized insights" based on CGM data and lifestyle habits, machine learning-based glucotype classification (documented in published research), and real-time actionable insights. Their published study references algorithmic analysis of glucose patterns.

### Clinical involvement model
- **Initial consultation:** In-clinic or video call with doctor + dietitian
- **Care team composition:** Dedicated doctor, dietitian, and fitness coach per patient
- **Ongoing engagement:** Digital coaching via app between clinic visits
- **Review cadence:** Scheduled based on program tier
- **Hours:** Mon-Fri 9am-6pm (closed weekends)

**Medical team includes** Dr. Sue-Anne Toh (Endocrinology), Dr. Kyle Tan (Preventive Medicine), Dr. Todd On (Family Medicine), Dr. Yeo Junjie (Family Medicine), plus 5+ dietitians and 2 fitness coaches.

## Conclusion: Key competitive insights for Thailand market entry

Novi Health represents a **well-funded, physician-founded competitor** with strong clinical credibility but relatively modest scale (~34 employees, thousands of patients). Their model combines traditional specialist medical care with technology-enabled coaching and CGM monitoring.

**Strengths to note:**
- Exceptional founder credentials from Singapore's public health system
- Published clinical outcomes in peer-reviewed journals
- Strong Google reviews (4.9/5 with 496+ reviews)
- Clear pricing transparency and tiered program structure
- CHAS/Medisave integration for local insurance

**Vulnerabilities:**
- App experience is a clear weakness (3.4/5 on Android)
- Limited social media scale (~2,700 Instagram followers)
- No visible influencer or ambassador strategy
- Singapore-only geographic footprint
- Supplements offering is minimal

**Pricing benchmarks for Thailand comparison:**
- Entry-level diabetes program: ~$299 SGD (~7,800 THB)
- Premium weight loss with GLP-1s: ~$469-1,199/month SGD (~12,200-31,200 THB/month)
- Comprehensive longevity assessment: ~$1,099 SGD (~28,600 THB)

Their "holistic, doctor-led, technology-enhanced" positioning and focus on "healthspan" and metabolic health root causes aligns directly with longevity/functional health positioning, making them a relevant competitive reference for Southeast Asian market strategy.
