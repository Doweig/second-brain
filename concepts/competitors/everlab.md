# Everlab Competitive Intelligence Brief

Australia's leading longevity clinic has raised **A$18 million** to scale an AI-powered preventive health platform, positioning itself as the premium choice for proactive health consumers. Founded in 2023 in Melbourne, Everlab combines **100+ biomarker testing** with telehealth doctor consultations and a sophisticated data visualization app. The company has processed over **1 million biomarkers** and serves corporate clients including BHP and KPMG, with entry pricing at **A$299** escalating to A$2,999 for comprehensive annual programs.

## Positioning and messaging

Everlab positions itself as **"Australia's leading personal longevity clinic"** targeting health-proactive consumers who want to detect disease years before symptoms appear. Their messaging centers on three core value propositions: early detection ("screen for 1,000+ diseases"), deep personalization ("tailored longevity solutions"), and historical data consolidation ("10+ years of medical records analyzed").

### Primary headlines and taglines

The homepage leads with **"Future of preventive health"** and **"Personalised longevity for modern life."** Supporting copy emphasizes: "Detect, prevent, and reverse disease risks with our advanced health check, cutting-edge diagnostics, and longevity experts." ### Target customer profiles

Website personas reveal targeting across age ranges and health concerns. Primary segments include new parents wanting comprehensive health checkups (Andrew, 34), individuals with family disease history seeking prevention (Eric, 57), and women navigating perimenopause with cancer family history (Vanessa, 42). Corporate positioning targets executive health assessments with clients including **BHP, KPMG, Culture AMP, Slice, and Amber**.

## Pricing architecture

Everlab employs a **tiered pricing model** with a low-commitment entry point designed to funnel customers toward comprehensive programs.

### Entry point and tier structure

The mandatory starting point is a **Health Baseline Risk Assessment at A$299**, which includes a 30-minute doctor consultation, 20 years of medical record gathering, AI risk analysis, and app access. This deposit is 100% refundable, reducing purchase friction.

| Tier | One-Off Price | Annual Price | Key Inclusions |
|------|---------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Essentials** | A$499 | A$799/year | 65 biomarkers, screens 800+ conditions, twice-yearly blood testing, digital dashboard |
| **Diagnostics** | A$900 | A$1,199/year | 80+ biomarkers, DEXA scan, heart health assessment, doctor review, intervention plan |
| **Protocol** (Premium) | A$2,700 | A$2,999/year | 100+ biomarkers, nutrition assessment, VO₂ max testing, physical assessment, 6-month retesting |

### Add-on services (à la carte)

Individual diagnostics range from A$49 (Biological Age test) to A$3,499 (Full Body MRI). Notable mid-tier add-ons include Hormone Panels at A$249, CT Calcium Score at A$699, Genetic Health Test at A$1,399, and CTCA Heart Scan at A$1,499. The modular structure enables customized programs based on individual risk profiles identified during baseline assessment.

### Upsell mechanics

The pricing architecture follows a classic **land-and-expand model**. After the A$299 baseline, doctors recommend specific diagnostics based on identified risks, naturally guiding customers toward higher tiers. The Protocol tier includes 6-month retesting, creating built-in re-engagement. Corporate pricing reportedly ranges from A$3,000 to A$15,000+ annually per participant.

## Product and service details

### Biomarker testing scope

Everlab offers testing across **19 medical categories**: Heart Health, Cancer Detection, Metabolic Function, Autoimmunity, Kidney Function, Liver Function, Hormone Health, Blood and Bone Marrow Function, Genetic Health, Body Composition (DEXA), Bone Health, Gut Health, Biological Age, Allergies, Heavy Metals, Muscle Health, Aerobic Capacity, Nutrition, and Electrolytes. The Protocol tier includes **100+ biomarkers** with screening capability for **1,000+ diseases**.

### Testing logistics

All testing requires **in-person visits** to partner facilities—Everlab does not offer at-home collection. The company operates through a network of **1,850+ diagnostic labs** across NSW, VIC, QLD, SA, ACT, and WA. Physical Everlab clinic locations exist in Melbourne (Flinders Lane), Sydney (York Street), Brisbane (Bowen Hills), and Perth (Lathlain), with additional testing handled through pathology partners like Melbourne Pathology (350+ collection centers in Victoria alone).

### Turnaround times

Initial testing typically begins within one week of the Health Strategy Consult. Blood work processing takes **3-5 business days**, with most members completing initial diagnostics within **2-4 weeks** of joining. Physical assessments take approximately 90 minutes at partner facilities. Next available appointments are typically within 3-4 days.

### Supplement offering

Everlab provides **personalized supplement recommendations only**—they do not sell supplements directly or operate a marketplace. Action plans include "targeted supplementation to address deficiencies or optimize specific biomarkers," but product procurement remains with the customer.

### Subscription model

The company offers **both one-time purchases and annual subscriptions**. The Essentials tier annual subscription (A$799/year) is explicitly "cancel anytime." Higher tiers are primarily positioned as one-off purchases with optional annual renewal. Payment plans are available for some programs.

## Technology and app features

### Mobile application

Everlab offers apps on both **iOS App Store and Google Play** with the following capabilities: historical results tracking with graphed visualization, custom reference ranges based on individual risk profiles, data consolidation from external health records, in-app test booking, progress tracking for biomarkers and habits, action plan management, and direct upload of past test results for automated analysis.

### AI and personalization claims

The company positions AI prominently in its value proposition. Marketing materials reference **"AI-powered insights on areas of risk and opportunity"** during baseline assessment and **"AI-interpreted biomarkers that reveal unique health patterns."** A July 2025 press release announced funding to "deliver scalable AI-powered preventive healthcare." The platform claims to reduce clinician review time from approximately 10 hours to under 10 minutes through automated clinical summaries. Technical specifics of AI models are not disclosed.

### Data consolidation capability

A differentiating feature is historical medical record gathering—Everlab claims to collect and analyze **20 years of Australian medical data** per member, consolidating approximately 80% of available records. This creates a "digital health avatar" combining medical history, lifestyle data, and family history for cross-analysis.

## Doctor and clinical involvement

### Clinical model

All consultations are delivered via **telehealth** by Australian-registered GPs with specialist expertise in early risk detection and longevity medicine. The clinical team includes Everlab doctors, Accredited Practising Dietitians, physiotherapists, coaches, and genetic counselors.

### Consultation structure

The Health Strategy Consult (30 minutes) establishes baseline risk assessment and diagnostic recommendations. Diagnostic Review consultations (45 minutes) cover results interpretation and action planning. Nutrition assessments (45 minutes) are conducted by dietitians, and physical assessments (90 minutes) occur at partner facilities. Progress reviews range from 15-30 minutes.

### GP relationship positioning

Everlab explicitly states: **"The Everlab program specialises in preventative care, and we are not a replacement of your existing GP relationship."** Doctors can prescribe medications if clinically necessary and make specialist referrals when needed.

Some users describe a "beta tester" feeling given the company's young age.

## Business model assessment

### Revenue model

Everlab operates a **hybrid one-time/subscription model** with strong upsell mechanics. Entry-level A$299 baseline assessments convert to programs ranging from A$499 to A$2,999, with add-on diagnostics providing additional revenue up to A$3,499 per test. Corporate contracts likely provide recurring revenue at premium rates (A$3,000-15,000+ annually per participant).

### Unit economics indicators

The telehealth-first model with partner lab network suggests capital-light operations. AI automation claims (reducing clinician review from 10 hours to 10 minutes) imply scalable economics. The **25% abnormal results rate** (1 in 4 members) creates strong clinical justification for comprehensive testing recommendations.

### Competitive moat elements

Key defensibility factors include: **1,850+ lab partnership network** creating geographic coverage, **proprietary historical data consolidation** differentiating from point-in-time competitors, **AI platform investment** from A$18M funding, **regulatory compliance** with Australian-registered GPs, and **brand positioning** as premium longevity provider.

## Strategic observations

### Strengths for competitive consideration

Everlab has established **strong brand positioning** in the Australian longevity market with premium perception ("If Apple designed a medical clinic"). The **comprehensive testing scope** (100+ biomarkers, 19 categories, 1,000+ disease screening) exceeds most competitors. **Exceptional customer satisfaction** (4.8-5.0 star ratings, 80+ NPS) validates product-market fit. The **partner lab model** enables geographic scale without capital-intensive clinic buildout.

### Potential vulnerabilities

The **telehealth-only consultation model** limits appeal to demographics preferring in-person care. **Third-party lab quality variance** outside major metros creates service consistency challenges. **No at-home testing option** requires customer travel to partner facilities. **Premium pricing** (A$2,999 for comprehensive program) limits addressable market. The company's **young age** (founded 2023) means limited operational track record.

### Implications for Thailand market entry

Everlab's model demonstrates consumer appetite for comprehensive preventive health programs in affluent Asia-Pacific markets. Their pricing structure—low-friction entry point (A$299) with tiered upsells—provides a validated template. The partner lab network model could translate to Thailand's fragmented healthcare infrastructure. Key differentiators to consider: at-home collection options (which Everlab lacks), in-person consultation availability, and localized pricing for Thai market economics. Their emphasis on **historical data consolidation** represents a genuine UX innovation worth noting.
