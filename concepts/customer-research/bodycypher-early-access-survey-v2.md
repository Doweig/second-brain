# BodyCypher — Early Access Survey Structure

*Last updated: February 12, 2026*
*Platform: Tally (https://tally.so/forms/ODa2op)*

---

## Overview

8 questions across 3 pages + confirmation page. Designed to qualify early access signups while collecting essential personalization and market data. All questions are required.

---

## Page 1 — Contact Info

**Header:** "Join Early Access"
**Subtext:** "We're building Thailand's first comprehensive blood panels with AI-powered action plans."

| # | Question | Type | Required |
|---|----------|------|----------|
| — | `source` | Hidden field | — |
| 1 | What should we call you? | Short text (first name) | Yes |
| 2 | Your email | Email | Yes |

---

## Page 2 — Health Goals

| # | Question | Type | Required | Options |
|---|----------|------|----------|---------|
| 3 | What matters most? | Multi-select | Yes | Energy & performance · Hormones & libido · Sleep & stress · Longevity / biological age · Heart & metabolic health · Immune support · Memory & focus · Skin, hair & nails · Bones, joints & muscles · Iron & nutrient levels · General curiosity — just want to know my numbers |
| 4 | Where do you usually get health information? | Multi-select | Yes | Doctor or clinic visits · Google / web search · YouTube health channels · Podcasts (Huberman, Attia, etc.) · ChatGPT or other AI tools · Social media (Instagram, TikTok, Reddit) · Friends, family, or colleagues · Health apps or wearables (Oura, Whoop, Apple Health) · Books or newsletters · I mostly don't — that's why I'm here |

## Page 4 — Confirmation

**Header:** "You're on the list."
**Subtext:** "Most health checkups tell you what's wrong. We tell you what to do about it."

**What happens next:**
- → Check your inbox — your welcome email is on the way
- → We'll reach out when we're ready to schedule your first test
- → Early access members get founding member pricing (locked in forever)

**Footer:** "Have questions? Reply to any email — a real human reads every one."
