## Cross-section notes (competitor feature patterns)
- Each category presents: (1) score gauge + trend, (2) short educational paragraph, (3) biomarker tiles with **status pills** (Optimized/Normal/Borderline High), (4) "View details" button, (5) recommendation count link.
- Multiple categories reuse the same biomarkers (e.g., cortisol appears in Cognition/Fitness/Gut Health/Hormone Balance; glucose/insulin/HbA1c across Sleep/Cognition/Metabolism/Gut).
- Categories can show "score not calculated" if the test panel lacks required markers; they still display biomarker tiles and recommendations.
