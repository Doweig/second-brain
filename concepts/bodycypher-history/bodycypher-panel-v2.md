# BodyCypher Blood Panel v2

*Updated February 18, 2026*
*Changes from v1: Dropped AA/EPA (C674), replaced S901 with individual packages, replaced Vitamin D2/D3 (S074) with Vitamin D total (C163), removed standalone Chromium (S042) — already covered in Toxic Metals Profile (S261)*

---

## List 1: Complete Biomarker Coverage (78 unique blood biomarkers)

### Hematology
**CBC (A010)** - 15 values:
- White Blood Cell Count (WBC)
- Red Blood Cell Count (RBC)
- Hemoglobin
- Hematocrit
- MCV, MCH, MCHC, RDW
- Platelet Count, MPV
- Neutrophils, Lymphocytes, Monocytes, Eosinophils, Basophils

**ESR (A080)** - 1 value:
- Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate

### Metabolic / Diabetes
- Fasting Glucose (C040)
- HbA1c (C880)
- Insulin (N710)

### Kidney
- Blood Urea Nitrogen (C530)
- Creatinine + eGFR (C521)

### Liver
**Liver Function Test (C071)** - 8 values:
- AST (SGOT), ALT (SGPT), Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP)
- Total Bilirubin, Direct Bilirubin
- Total Protein, Albumin, Globulin

Plus standalone:
- GGT (C590)

### Lipids / Cardiovascular
**Lipid Set (C018)** - 4 values:
- Total Cholesterol, Triglycerides, HDL, LDL

Individual:
- Apolipoprotein A-1 + Apolipoprotein B (C912)
- Lipoprotein(a) (C685)

### Inflammation
- CRP High Sensitivity (P205)

### Electrolytes
**Electrolytes (C004)** - 4 values:
- Sodium, Potassium, Chloride, CO2/Bicarbonate

Individual:
- Calcium (C100)

### Thyroid (4 markers)
- TSH (N010)
- Free T4 (N030)
- Free T3 (N025)
- Anti-TPO / Thyroid Peroxidase Ab (T160)

### Hormones / Growth (5 markers)
- Testosterone (N305)
- Cortisol (N530)
- DHEAS (N320)
- IGF-1 (N745)
- IGFBP-3 (N741)

### Antioxidants
**Antioxidant 10 HPLC (S072)** - 10 values:
- Vitamin A (Retinol)
- Vitamin E (gamma-Tocopherol)
- Vitamin E (alpha-Tocopherol)
- Lutein + Zeaxanthin
- beta-Cryptoxanthin
- Lycopene
- alpha-Carotene
- beta-Carotene
- Coenzyme Q10
- Vitamin C (Ascorbic acid)

### Minerals
**Antioxidant Minerals (S912)** - 3 values:
- Selenium, Copper, Zinc

Individual:
- Magnesium (C340)

*Note: Chromium covered via Toxic Metals Profile (S261)*

### Vitamins / Nutrients
- Ferritin (N110)
- Vitamin B12 (N950)
- Folate (N127)
- Vitamin D total / 25-OH (C163)

### Toxicology
**Toxic Metals Profile (S261)** - 7 values:
- Chromium (Cr), Cobalt (Co), Manganese (Mn), Nickel (Ni)
- Mercury (Hg), Lead (Pb), Cadmium (Cd)

### Urine
**Urine Examination (D111)**:
- pH, Specific Gravity, Protein, Glucose, Ketones, Blood, Bilirubin, Urobilinogen, Nitrite, Leukocyte Esterase + microscopy

---

**Total: 78 unique blood biomarkers across 33 test codes (+ urine panel)**
