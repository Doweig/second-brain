# The Problem

## You leave the clinic with numbers. Not answers.

Every year, millions of people in Thailand walk out of a clinic holding a PDF full of numbers they don't understand.

**Hospital checkups (฿3,000–15,000):** You're herded through an assembly line. You leave with a booklet, a 3-minute consult, and zero guidance on what to actually change.

**Premium clinics (฿30,000–1,000,000+):** Better doctors. But the advice is delivered orally — and within two weeks, it's gone. You're left with: *"something about creatinine... drink more water?"*

**DIY (ChatGPT + lab PDF):** Already happening — 1 in 4 adults under 30 upload blood results to AI. But it's unstructured, inconsistent, and has no clinical oversight.

**The result:** PDFs pile up. Insights decay. People stay frustrated.

93% of Thai consumers report increased health consciousness — but only **4.9%** get annual checkups. The demand exists. The product doesn't.

# The Insight

## The gap isn't testing quality. It's what happens after.

Thailand has world-class lab infrastructure. 62+ JCI-accredited hospitals. N Health processing 20M+ tests per year. The raw materials are excellent.

**The failure is in the last mile:**

- **No synthesis.** Each biomarker gets siloed advice. "Eat more fish" for triglycerides + "avoid dairy" for cholesterol = confusion.
- **No persistence.** Oral advice disappears. PDFs are filed and forgotten. Nothing is designed to be referenced weekly.
- **No continuity.** Each checkup is an isolated event. Nobody tracks trends across tests.
- **No specificity.** "Exercise more" is not an action plan. Dosing, timing, food quantities, supplement stacks — that's what changes behavior.

We studied 200+ user reviews across 12+ platforms and 7 international competitors. **The interpretation gap is the #1 pain point — across every platform, every user type, and every market.**

# The Solution

## The action plan is the product.

A membership that combines comprehensive blood testing with structured, doctor-reviewed guidance you can actually follow.

**1. Book** — Blood draw at home. No hospital queues. A nurse comes to you, fasting-friendly, early morning.

**2. Test** — 78 biomarkers across hormones, thyroid, metabolic, inflammation, antioxidants, toxic metals, and micronutrients. Not just "are you sick" markers — optimization markers.

**3. Understand** — AI generates a structured action plan, reviewed and approved by a licensed doctor:
- Executive summary (your health in 2 minutes)
- Per-biomarker education (tap any result to learn why it matters)
- Dietary recommendations (specific foods, quantities)
- Supplement protocol (exact dosing, timing, evidence)
- Doctor's Notes (in the doctor's own words — the human touch)

**4. Act** — Supplement recommendations with a pre-built cart on our marketplace. No guessing at pharmacy shelves.

**5. Track** — Retest in 3–6 months. Visualize trends. See what's working. Adjust the protocol.

**The moment it clicks:** *"This is what my doctor told me — but written down, and I can actually follow it."*

# What We've Learned

## Real insights from real work. Not slide deck theory.

**"Healthy people have boring blood results."**
Most of our target demo's full panels will come back normal. The nutrition-focused package (~฿9K, fewer markers) is more likely to surface actionable findings — especially vitamin D deficiency (near-universal in Thailand). This is the easier marketing story and a natural supplements upsell.

**"Ratios matter more than raw values."**
Dr. Chawapon: *"The ratios tell you what the real problem is."* HOMA-IR, triglyceride/HDL, cortisol/DHEA — these calculated ratios catch metabolic patterns that individual markers miss. We compute 15+ ratios automatically.

**"The #1 objection is price."**
Not "I don't believe in blood testing." It's "that seems expensive." Sub-฿10K breaks a psychological barrier. Everything above 5 digits feels like a luxury purchase.

**"Supplements transparency beats premium margins."**
Competitors claiming 50-70% supplement margins get roasted in reviews. Customers look up prices. We sell at honest retail margins on our own marketplace — and win on trust.
