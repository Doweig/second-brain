# BodyCypher — Legal Master Document Braindump

Voice memo from Guillaume, 2026-03-10. Two requests plus a phased product launch framework.

Create a master legal document (under `strategy/`) that consolidates:
- All legal questions, classified by category

- Sources: [[art-tanapote|Tanapote]]/[[ipc-international|IPC]], [[acclime-thailand|Acclime]] (quotation pending), AI research
- Rating/confidence level per answer
- Cross-reference where sources agree vs. disagree
- Identify holes that need further investigation
- Living document — updated as new opinions come in, until every question reaches resolution

## Request 2: Phased Product Launch with Legal Gates

Chunk the legal work into stages matching the product lifecycle. Each stage has a legal confidence gate — only release to customers when that stage reaches 100% legal clarity.

### Proposed Stages

**Stage 1 — Entity & Legitimacy (PRESSING)**
- Legal entity incorporated
- Work visa for Guillaume
- Website with public-facing identity (CEO title)
- Ability to do podcasts, public speaking as company representative
- Minimal legal requirements — just need to exist legally

**Stage 2 — Blood Test Guidance (Free / Registration-Only)**
- No money exchanged, registration required
- Recommend blood test packages (longevity panel, supplement check, etc.)
- Link to recommended labs — user clicks, gets own quotation, arranges own test
- BodyCypher does NOT provide the test, just guidance
- Legal requirement likely much lower than full service

**Stage 3 — Nurse Service (Paid)**
- Customer pays through BodyCypher
- Nurse dispatched to home for blood draw
- Results delivered by email
- No analysis provided yet — just the logistics/convenience layer

**Stage 4 — Basic Analysis (Range Flagging)**
- Show which markers are out of range using proprietary optimal ranges
- No recommendations — just highlight what's interesting
- Possible positioning: "for research purposes only"
- Customer can copy-paste into their own AI tool for follow-up
- Keep proprietary ranges somewhat private

**Stage 5 — Clinician Notes (Written)**
- Doctor reviews results and provides written recommendations
- With or without AI assistance (depends on SaMD classification)
- No video call yet — text-based doctor review only

**Stage 6 — Video Consultation**
- Doctor call through [[decypher|the app]]
- Telemedicine license/partnership requirements
- Or delegate to clinic entity to avoid tech company needing telemedicine capability

**Stage 7 — Supplement Recommendations**
- AI or doctor recommends specific supplements
- No selling — just recommendations with disclaimers
- "Research purpose only" or similar positioning

**Stage 8 — Supplement Sales (Third-Party Fulfillment)**
- Customer pays through BodyCypher, third party ships
- Or partner with e-commerce platform
- Or build own e-commerce integrated into the app
- FDA registration, labeling, pharmacy licensing questions

**Stage 9 — Custom Supplements (Daily Sachets)**
- Pre-packaged daily doses customized per customer
- Morning/afternoon split dosing
- Manufacturing/repackaging licenses (GMP/HACCP)
- Final product vision: test → plan → custom supplements delivered

## Key Insight

Each stage can be independently assessed for legal readiness. The product ships incrementally as legal confidence hits 100% per stage, rather than waiting for everything to be resolved before launching anything.
